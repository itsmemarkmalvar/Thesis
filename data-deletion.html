BINIBABY Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: December 28, 2024

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the BINIBABY app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select "Account"
  4. Tap "Delete Account and Data"
  5. Follow the confirmation steps

Option 2: Email Request

  1. Send an email to mark_malvar321@yahoo.com
  2. Subject line: "BINIBABY - Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include in your email:
    • Your full name
    • Registered email address
    • Reason for deletion (optional)

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Account information
  • Baby profiles and related data
  • Growth and development records
  • Photos uploaded to the app
  • Usage history and preferences

Timeline

We will process your deletion request within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Important Notes

  • Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • If you're using Facebook login, we'll also remove the app's connection to your Facebook account
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes

Questions?

If you have any questions about the data deletion process, please contact us at mark_malvar321@yahoo.com
